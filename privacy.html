Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Control.

At QuickCopy, your privacy is our top priority. We believe in transparency and giving you full control over your information.

What We Collect

  • Snippets: Your pasted text and links are stored securely in the cloud via Firebase Firestore.
  • Authentication: We use Firebase Auth to manage your account and secure your data.

Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption protocols provided by Google Cloud (Firebase). Your snippets are private to your account and cannot be accessed by other users.

Your Rights

You have the right to delete your account and all associated snippets at any time. This action is permanent and wipes all data from our cloud servers.

⚠️ Data Deletion

Once deleted, your snippets cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have backups of important information.
