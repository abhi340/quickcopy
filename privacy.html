Data Sovereignty

Your Data, Your Control.

At QuickCopy Pro, we believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. Our ecosystem is built from the ground up to ensure that you remain the sole owner of your information.

1. Zero-Knowledge Collection

We only store what is absolutely necessary for the app to function. This includes your snippets (encrypted in transit and at rest) and basic account metadata required for synchronization.

2. Industry-Standard Protection

Your data is secured using Google's global infrastructure. We implement Firebase Security Rules that ensure only your specific authenticated UID can read or write to your data silo.

3. Total Data Erasure

We provide a "Nuclear Option" in your settings. Clicking "Delete Account" instantly and permanently wipes your profile and all associated snippets from our cloud database. There are no backups kept after this action.

⚠️ Safety Notice

QuickCopy Pro does not sell, trade, or share your data with third-party advertisers. Your clipboard is your private space.
